About The Keepsake Initiative

The Keepsake Initiative began with one simple belief: Hospitals can be places of hope. Founded by students who experienced hospital life firsthand, we bring music, storytelling, and human connection to children facing long-term medical challenges.

Our mission

We bring joy, creativity, and comfort to children facing chronic or life-threatening illnesses. Through music, storytelling, and meaningful experiences, we create personalized keepsakes that families can treasure forever.

What we are not

We are not a wish-granting organization like Make-A-Wish. We do not replace clinical child life services. We partner with hospitals and child life departments to create uplifting experiences.
